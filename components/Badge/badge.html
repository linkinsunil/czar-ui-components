Badge

Badges are used to show status of the user (online, or offline). It can also be used as notification counter badge.

Badge on Icons

                    
          <button class="badge-btn mx-1">
            <i class="fas fa-home"></i>
            <span class="badge-count bg-brand">1</span>
          </button>
  
          <button class="badge-btn mx-1">
            <i class="fas fa-shopping-cart"></i>
            <span class="badge-count bg-brand">3+</span>
          </button>
  
          <button class="badge-btn mx-1">
            <i class="fas fa-bell"></i>
            <span class="badge-count bg-amber-500">2</span>
          </button>

Badge on Avatars

                    
          <div class="badge">
            <img class="avatar" src="..." alt="..." />        
            <span class="badge-status bg-lime-500"></span>
          </div>

          <div class="badge">
            <img class="avatar" src="..." alt="..." />        
            <span class="badge-status bg-red-500"></span>
          </div>
  
          <div class="badge">
            <img class="avatar" src="..." alt="..." />
            <span class="badge-status bg-slate-400"></span>
          </div>
